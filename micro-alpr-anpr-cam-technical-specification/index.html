Product roadmap

We are actively working on improving the camera functionality for ALPR/ANPR and other traffic-related applications.

 

The following important features are in the development pipeline:

  • night time operation
  • processing multiple lanes and directions simultaneously
  • self-calibration

 

* – detection and OCR of EU number plates

** – 0.017Wh per vehicle detection figure is based on processing over 4000 vehicles with a 70Wh battery pack and will change depending on the situation.

 

Do you have a software feature or a hardware modification you need for your application?

Let us know or open an issue in one of our open source Github repositories.
