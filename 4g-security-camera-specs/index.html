Smart

Sensorable 4G cams can do live image processing, connect to sensors and control remote lights at the same time. Add to that a high-end 12MP image sensor with full colour night vision.

Small

Only slightly bigger than a smartphone and running on an internal battery it has more computing power than a Pentium 4 computer.

Rugged

Designed for outdoor use, it can be placed anywhere outside or in harsh environments without any additional enclosures.
